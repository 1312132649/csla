I don't have any instructions for setting up a data portal with a custom Windows service or with AppFabric or with WAS.

But I don't worry about that too much, or haven't anyway, since there are just two things to worry about:

  1. get all your DLL files in the right folder
  2. get your web.config file set up correctly

You can find information on MSDN and in books on how to work with WAS in general. And the web.config information from my books is the same for WAS as it is for IIS/ASP.NET.

JonnyBee replied on Wednesday, April 18, 2012

Hi Alexander,

the best options I know of is:

  • CodeComplete, Commercial, http://eurowisesoft.com with T4 based templates.
  • CodeSmith, Commercial, http://codesmithtools.com with codesmith templates.
  • CslaGenFork, OpenSource, http://cslagenfork.codeplex.com with codesmith templates (last free codsmith version 2.6 used for generation)

All support CSLA 4 and have been released recently.
Download the trial version/full version and test them to see which product fits best for your needs.

And please post your findings to us.

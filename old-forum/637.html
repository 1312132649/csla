Learn how to work within your project, connect to your project using Team Explorer and more at http://www.codeplex.com/Wiki/View.aspx?ProjectName=CodePlex&title=CodePlex%20Help%20Wiki. 

 

Please log any feature requests or bugs in the Issue Tracker which is in the CodePlex Information & Discussion project on the CodePlex site at the following URL:

http://www.codeplex.com/Wiki/View.aspx?ProjectName=CodePlex

 

Note: You have to be logged in to submit the information.

 

All ideas are considered during weekly triages and issues get addressed based on priority over time. Logging your suggestion to the Issue Tracker allows you to monitor progress regarding your work item and make modifications to it, if needed.

 

Thank you for choosing CodePlex!

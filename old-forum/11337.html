JonnyBee replied on Saturday, April 28, 2012

Hi Seth,

We do not have a sample that runs in Mono and calls a Windows service in the Samples download.

CSLA 4.3.10 introduced a new custom serialization engine, read more here:
http://www.lhotka.net/Article.aspx?area=4&id=1c287e08-d428-4c27-b538-d588e3c55775 
and using this serialization and basicHttpBinding on the DataPortal (serverside) there is no indication that this
should NOT work with a Mono client.

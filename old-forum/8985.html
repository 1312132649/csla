RockfordLhotka posted on Monday, May 24, 2010

CSLA 4 Beta 2 is now available at http://www.lhotka.net/cslanet/download.aspx. This includes support for .NET 4 and Silverlight 4.

Beta 2 includes quite a number of enhancements and bug fixes from Beta 1, mostly in response to feedback from members of this forum. Thank you for your valuable help!!

Big changes include:

  • New PrivateField relationship type for properties, so ReadProperty/LoadProperty work automatically with private backing fields
  • INotifyDataErrorInfo support in Silverlight
  • Templates and snippets are updated for CSLA 4
  • More samples are updated (but we still have a long way to go...)

Please download the new version and give it a try. As always, your feedback is incredibly valuable; please post any findings here on the forum.

Thank you!

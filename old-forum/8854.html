RockfordLhotka posted on Wednesday, April 28, 2010

I know I said there wouldn't be another beta - but there is - one last beta of 3.8.3. Changes include:

  • Fixed issue with using fully qualified type names for an IsInRoleProvider
  • Back-ported TriggerAction control from CSLA 4 into Csla.Wpf and Csla.Silverlight namespaces

The TriggerAction control is necessary so my MVVM videos can use a consistent technique between 3.8 and 4 in terms of invoking methods in the viewmodel based on UI events.

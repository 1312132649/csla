Magenic is the corporate patron of CSLA .NET, and a consulting company. If you are looking for consulting/mentoring, Magenic is the place to go. You can send an email to "sales" at magenic.com to get in touch with a Magenic sales person.

Dunn Training is the official training company for CSLA .NET, and they offer 3 and 5 day training classes.

You can get information about both organizations from www.lhotka.net/cslanet/faq

The FAQ will also tell you where to get other good information, such as the Expert 2008 Business Objects book, and the CSLA .NET for Silverlight training videos.

Michael Hildner replied on Monday, July 24, 2006

I think you do mean data sources. Click on Data | Show Data Sources to show your data sources. You can create a new one from there, choosing your business object. Make sure you have a form open in design view, then you can drag your data source properties onto the form.

If you click the drop down arrow on the right of the data source, you can change the control it will use.

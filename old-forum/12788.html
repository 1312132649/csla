Hi,

RemotingProxy was removed in 2012 for CSLA 4.5.x release.

See: http://www.lhotka.net/cslabugs/edit_bug.aspx?id=1071  

You could probably create a private build/nuget packages of the latest CSLA with the missing RemotingProxy or move onto WCF or the Http proxy.

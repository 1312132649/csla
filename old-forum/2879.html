skagen00 replied on Wednesday, May 16, 2007

Make sure that if you purchase something that it is the Expert Business Objects 2005 book and not the e-book. The e-book (which I think was 24.95) is to document changes made after the book was released.

Even if you don't end up programming with CSLA, the book is worth it - no question. It was one of the more informative books I made the choice to pick up. But if you only buy the e-book, you'll probably be disappointed because it won't do you much good if you haven't read the core published book.

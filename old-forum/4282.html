Hi,

#1 - You really have to buy the book if you want to understand what CSLA is. Rocky originally wrote CSLA as a sample framework (to sell the book) and it has changed over the years to a fully supported one.

#2 - CSLA is free. No license required.

#3 - Your hardware is fine for CSLA 2.x. Rocky is about to publish CSLA 3.5.

#4 - After reading the CSLA 2005 book you should get the eBook directly from Rocky to see the enhancements made since then.

#5 - Then read this forum to see his latest thinking.

#6 - Rocky provides a sample app named Project Tracker to demo the features of CSLA.

If you are writing a line of business app then, in my opinion, CSLA is probably the best framework to use.

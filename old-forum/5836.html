RockfordLhotka replied on Thursday, November 20, 2008

Given constraints on Nermin’s schedule (he’s doing the VS templates and such on his own time) it is unlikely that the VS templates and MSI will be complete for 3.6.0.

 

While that’s unfortunate, these elements aren’t critical to the release. The primary concern I have right now is making sure the 3.6.0 release is out in sync with the Expert C# 2008 Business Objects book. These elements aren’t covered in the book, and so they are purely optional from that perspective.

 

From a release gate perspective, only cslacs is truly critical. ProjectTracker already matches the book and is frozen, and I’ll only change cslacs for show-stopping bugs at this point.

 

Which doesn’t mean other issues shouldn’t be reported – just that they’ll be addressed in 3.6.x, not 3.6.0.

 

Rocky

I expect 3.5 to be out of beta in early to mid-April. So in 2-4 weeks basically. Several people/organizations on this forum are using Beta 2 and are providing some awesome feedback (thank you guys!!!), and I want to allow enough time for them (or you, if you'd like) to find any major issues so I can address them.

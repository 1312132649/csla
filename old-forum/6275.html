Fel replied on Tuesday, February 10, 2009

I was missing FactoryParameters.Clear() statement when I called CslaDataProvider

            CslaDataProvider provider = this.Resources["xxx"] as CslaDataProvider;

            provider.FactoryParameters.Clear()                

            provider.FactoryParameters.Add(SelectedCCId);

            provider.FactoryMethod = "YYY";

            provider.Refresh();

Position
We are looking for software developers and architects to join our team of engineers who are working on both our current and next generation products.  These initiatives are creative, rewarding and challenging, and require the types of individuals who enjoy developing software and solving real world problems, making a difference both from a business perspective and a social perspective.  Engineers are provided with top end equipment and have the tools and technologies needed to thrive.  All positions will report to the Director of Engineering.

Company
PeopleCube is a leading provider of on-demand software solutions that help the world's leading organizations integrate individuals and resources into the business process to improve productivity, increase revenue and reduce environmental impact and costs.  As a Microsoft Gold Certified partner and a company repeatedly experiencing annual double digit growth, PeopleCube has both the resources and opportunities to provide engineers with exposure to new technologies, challenges and a variety of career paths.  PeopleCube has US offices in Waltham, MA, Tucson, AZ as well as Grand Rapids and Traverse City, MI.

Responsibilities
You will be working as part of a team which includes Product Management, Business Analysts, QA and other engineers, and will be responsible for developing web based software applications in an Agile methodology.  Given our broad product suite, you will have opportunities to work in various segments of our end user products.  Possibilities include UI development, database development, integration with 3rd party applications (including mobile and wireless technologies) and middleware development (business objects, services, messaging, web services).

Job Skills
• Ability to multi-task and work with team to meet deadlines
• Strong verbal and written communication skills
• Strong analytical skills, attention to detail - a problem solver
• Creativity and willingness to think outside the box

Required Technical Skills
• Knowledgeable with best practices and design patterns
• Microsoft Development Languages: C#, Visual Studio, .NET Framework

• Web Based Software Development: ASP.NET, SQL Server, HTML, JavaScript

   - and/or -

• Familiarity with CSLA Framework for .NET

Beneficial Technical Skills
• Experience in developing highly optimized, transaction-based web applications
• Experience in developing scheduling and time-based applications
• Developing software in an ISV environment
• Familiarity with XML and Web Services
• Familiarity with AJAX on .NET
• Advanced knowledge of SQL (stored procedures, views, triggers)
• Experience in developing MS Outlook solutions including Office Business Apps (OBA's)

Other Notes
• Bachelors degree or higher in Computer Science or related field or related experience preferred
• Agile development concepts a plus
• Certifications such as MSCE, MSCA, MSDBA, MCAD, MCSD a plus
• On-site full-time employment; no remote developers
• U.S. citizenship is required for this position

If you are interested in this position, please send your resume and cover letter to grcareers at peoplecube.com.  You can learn more about PeopleCube at our website, http://www.peoplecube.com/.  PeopleCube is an Equal Opportunity employer that values the strength that diversity brings to the workplace.  No phone calls or agencies please.

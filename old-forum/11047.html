Hi,

Please check that your object structure does not have circular references.

Any relationship that is not "owning" reference must use private backing field and have [NonSerialized] and [NotUndoable] attributes.

This is described in Rocky's books and the Using CSLA4 Creating Business Objects ebook. (recommended).

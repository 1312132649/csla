triplea replied on Tuesday, June 24, 2008

Yes the book covers the 2.0 version of CSLA. The "Version 2.1 handbook" expands a bit from where "C# Business Objects 2005" stops and covers some of the enhancements and finally the "Version 3.0" book covers the .Net 3.0 features used by CSLA (as well as some other enhancements).
"C# Business Objects 2005" is probably still the best place to start but keep an eye on the release notes for the 3.x versions of CSLA as certain important fixes/enhancements have been made.

JonnyBee replied on Saturday, August 18, 2012

Nope,

BusinessBindingListBase is the BusinessListBase from Csla 3.x.

Same goes for ReadOnlyBindingListBase and DynamicBindingListBase.

The list classes in 4.x inherits from ObserableCollection and works better wit Xaml (WPF/SL) whereas the "old" classes that inherit from BindingList is the only ones that work well in WindowsForms.

This change was done in CSLA 4.0

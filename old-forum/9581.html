AFAIK, Rocky is still working on a WP7-compatible version of CSLA.  I don't think he's finished yet, though - at least he hasn't said he is.  Nor do I know of any official WP7 drops, even in alpha form.

If nothing else, you should be able to look on SVN and see if he's made anything available.  I'm sure any code that's out there is "use at your own risk", but it would at least give you some idea of where he's going.

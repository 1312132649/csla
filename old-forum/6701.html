Sorry All,

I was a bit worried yesterday as I could not figure out what was going on.  I had never had this issue before in my dealings with CSLA.  Back in November, I installed DevExpress CodeRush and DevExpress Refactor! Pro for another project and left it installed when I returned to my CSLA project.  Well it appears that the CodeRush installation was the culprit.  Once removed, normal editor use for CSLA could continue.  DevExpress has some really nice components, but I guess their IDE integration stuff may have a bug or two.  They have been fairly responsive on other issues; I will see how responsive they are to this one.

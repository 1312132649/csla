Hi all,

 

I find SmartDate quite useful. But I thought I could improve it.

Besides the standard

  • Yesterday or "y" or "-"
  • Today or "t" or "." (dot)
  • Tomorrow or "tom" or "+"

I added

  • "<" to enter the same day of last month
  • ">" to enter the same day of next month
  • "-nn" to enter nn days before the current date
  • "+nn" to enter nn days after the current date
  • ".nn" (dot nn) to enter nn day of the current month
  • "<nn" to enter nn day of last month
  • ">nn" to enter nn day of next month
  • "nn" to enter nearest nn day (if today is the 25th, the nearest 5th will be next month, although the nearest 15th was this same month)

I had to change SmartDate.cs. I enclose the changed file as well as the diff file. SmartDate.cs will go on the Csla folder.

Rocky, can you consider including this change on the next release?

What do I need to do to have tools like propertyStatus and TriggerAction show in the Controls Toolbox?

RockfordLhotka replied on Friday, August 06, 2010

Right-click on the toolbox and select Choose Items - then browse to the appropriate assembly and add it.

Before doing that, you'll probably want to right-click on the toolbox and add a new section/region/whatever-they-call-it so your Csla items are in a Csla area.

When I save the view, my VM does the following:

Model.ApplyEdit();
Model.MyBaseObject = await Model.MyBaseObject.SaveAsync();
Model.MyListBaseObject = await Model.MyListBaseObject.SaveAsync();

What am I missing? Thanks.

For anyone else...

In lieu of <ContentControl x:Name="DataFieldsPanel"/> in XAML

<ContentControl x:Name="DataFieldsPanel">
                <ContentControl.Template>
                    <ControlTemplate>
                        <Grid>
                            <ContentPresenter />
                        </Grid>
                    </ControlTemplate>
                </ContentControl.Template>
            </ContentControl>

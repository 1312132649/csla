rfcdejong posted on Monday, July 13, 2009

I remember that i hate the designer because of the performance, but sometimes it's easy to know how your grid, stackpanel and some changes in code has a visual effect by just looking at the designer. But in Silverlight 3 RTW it seems they removed the designer completely, this because not to delay Silverlight 3 outroll.

Gladly it isn't gone, a preview designer is still available, but just hidden.

When u have an xaml open in silverlight:

- move your mouse down in the xaml workspace;
- there is a very small spot where your cursor becommes a vertical splitter:
- move the splitter up and an disabled designer becomes visible;
- hit "reload the designer"

There a limited designer becomes available, just for previewing :)

On the CSLAcontrib web site there's a tab for news. That tab automatically displays any top-level posts (first post in a thread) from the CSLA .NET Contrib forum.

One good use for this, is by contributors to CSLAcontrib. As you add or update content in the project, you can put a top-level post in the forum to let people know what you've done and how they can use your new content.

Unfortunately comments to posts don't appear in the news tab, but there is a link beneath each news item so people can easily navigate here to see the full thread.

RockfordLhotka replied on Tuesday, February 09, 2010

The forum is very stylable. If you'd like to take up the task of restyling it I'd surely welcome the help :)

The problem with the old version of the forum is that it didn't support modern browsers, had trouble pasting code, had a terrible search engine, had no way to recover passwords, no way to remove old/bad users, no spam filtering, an unreliable email subsystem (I had bat jobs running mutliple times a day to restart the email services), etc.

In short, the old version of the software was probably very good in 2004 or so, but after 6 years it was getting pretty unmanageable and would soon become unusable as browsers continue to evolve.

Other than visual style, all the content, posts and people are still here - it is the same forum and the same community.

The only thing we're missing at this point is inbound email. Sadly we were unable to get that feature working this past weekend due to a licensing issue, and I'm still waiting to hear back from Telligent on how to resolve that...

This is not really a technical post - but I think it is broadly useful, so here goes.

The forum software has decent search capabilities. However, it doesn't search www.lhotka.net, nor my blog.

Google, on the other hand, indexes all of the lhotka.net domain quite nicely - including the forum, my blog and www.lhotka.net.

You can go here

http://www.lhotka.net/Search.aspx

to perform a Google-powered search of everything within the lhotka.net domain.

Sometimes this can provide answers that you won't find by just searching the forum.

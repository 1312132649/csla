RockfordLhotka replied on Monday, October 19, 2009

The way it will work is similar to the SL series, but not quite the same.

 

There’ll be a 6 part “core” series:

 

1.       Introduction

2.       Object stereotypes

3.       Data access

4.       Using the data portal (2- and 3-tier)

5.       Business and validation rules

6.       Authentication/Authorization

 

I plan to sell that as a unit, much like the SL series.

 

Then there’ll be individual video segments for different interface technologies. They’ll be released and sold separately, and will probably come out in this order:

 

1.       WPF

2.       ASP.NET MVC

3.       Windows Forms

4.       ASP.NET Web Forms

5.       WCF Services

 

These will be sold individually.

Marjon1 replied on Tuesday, January 18, 2011

I'm not trying to be rude here, but to offer any assistance we need more than a cry for help. Trying to learn how CSLA wants you to develop can be difficult and is not something that will just come to you overnight, but over time it will be make more and more sense as you begin to use it.

The training videos that are available make the information much easier to absorb, compared to the book. The books are an excellent resources, but trying to learn the framework just from the books alone can be a frustrating and time consuming process. The sample applications are also a good place to learn some of the quirks of the newer UI's (silverlight / WPF).

If you can give us some more information about a specific problem you are having, we are happy to point you in the right direction.
